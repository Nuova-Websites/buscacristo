Meet Our Missionaries

Sharing the Gospel of Jesus Christ

Our full-time missionaries are dedicated young men and women who have chosen to serve the Lord for 18-24 months. They come from all over the world to share the message of Jesus Christ and His restored gospel.

These missionaries are here to help you learn about our beliefs, answer your questions, and provide spiritual guidance. They offer free lessons about Jesus Christ, the Book of Mormon, and how families can be together forever.
